Brief History

I've lived in Utah County my entire life, I love it here. Growing up I had a natural talent for drawing and sketching and a love for computers and games. These interests have grown into a passion for art and technology.

In 2006 I started a career in manufacturing for US Synthetic, a company which supplies synthetic diamonds drill bits for the oil and gas industry. I worked with mechanical and chemical engineers in various R&D groups for nearly 10 years.
While working full time I attended Utah Valley University at night studying Technology Management and Engineering Design.

In 2013 I designed and developed my first app for the iPhone. It was a vector based side scrollering game called Tricky Trout, it was such a fun and challenging experience that I knew my career path had suddenly changed.
